Early Warning System — Domestic Violence Risk Predictor

Apr 2026

FamilyGuard is a Machine Learning risk triage tool that helps social workers assess domestic violence referrals in under 30 seconds. Replacing a reactive system that only flagged cases after a crisis.

Logistic Regression 97% Recall 0.86 ROC-AUC 4 archetypes
  • Logistic Regression have zero train-test discrepancy, prioritising generalisation to new families over marginal accuracy gains. A deliberate trade-off when real families are at stake
  • Engineered Ecological Risk Index, Gender Power Imbalance Flag, Recidivism Risk Score
  • Clustered families into 4 intervention archetypes mapped to relevant support agencies
  • Live demo presented to panel. Bridged model methodology and policy for non-technical audience
